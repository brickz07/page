volt.fm You can see your top artists, songs, and albums but also more nuanced data like number of players, most active hours for listening, and number of plays.

The tool will make you a playlist of that genre or can generate a playlist of songs of a random genre.
MusicTaste.Space Allows you to compare your listening with another Spotify user. Just send a friend the link on the homepage and it will show you all the overlap between your top songs and top artists.
Receiptify Transforms your favorite songs into a cute little receipt of your musical taste. Choose your top tracks of the last month, last six months, or of all time.
